About BoBAFEST

Borno Book and Arts Festival is the maiden festival of arts and creativity happening in Borno State, celebrating arts, culture, storytelling and creativity in the state. The festival presents activities in form of panel discussions, workshops, poetry and local musical performances, art exhibition, cultural display, and book drives. The maiden theme “Narratives”, seeks to have discussions around issues that promotes positive narratives of Borno state, its people, creativity, culture and literature while erasing negative narratives such as the extremists narratives that have bedevilled the state. While creating a safe space for creatives, professionals and thinkers to dialogue across changing the narratives of Borno as a recovering community in areas of education, arts, tech, entrepreneurship, politics, culture, media, health, development and many more subjects.

Where
