About BoBAFEST

Borno Book and Arts Festival (BoBaFest) is a celebration of art, culture, storytelling, and creativity in Borno State, Nigeria. The maiden edition was held in November, 2022. The maiden festival's theme was "Narratives,” which aimed to change the narrative about the stories of Borno. The maiden festival included panel discussions, creative writing workshop, poetry readings, art exhibitions, art exhibition, and book chats.

This year’s theme is REIMAGINING Tomorrow which is aimed at being a platform for building resilience, enhancing access to quality education and protection for children, healing, cultural revitalization, and shaping a brighter future for Borno State which includes all aspects of development.
